No covalent linkage was observed between AGS and the enzyme. Instead, the ligand was accommodated by an oxyanion hole formed by the backbone amide nitrogens of Gly47, Leu48 and Gly49 in chain D. The carbonyl oxygen O1B of AGS was positioned 2.89 Å from the main-chain N of Gly47/D, 2.97 Å from the main-chain N of Leu48/D and 3.49 Å from the main-chain N of Gly49/D, consistent with stabilization of a tetrahedral adduct.

A network of direct hydrogen bonds was formed between AGS600/L and both backbone and side-chain atoms in the active site. The side-chain NH2 of Arg156/D donated a hydrogen bond to O3G of AGS (3.39 Å). The side chains of Arg203/D engaged two ligand oxygens: NH2 to O2A (3.11 Å) and NH1 to O3B (3.23 Å) as well as NH1 to O3G (3.21 Å). The main-chain carbonyl O of Ile14/D accepted a hydrogen bond from the exocyclic amine N6 of AGS (3.19 Å). Two main-chain NH interactions were observed: the N of Gly47/D with O3B (2.95 Å) and the N of Leu48/D with O1B (2.97 Å). Additional hydrogen bonds were formed by residue Thr51/D, where the main-chain N interacted with O1A (3.48 Å) and the side-chain OG1 with O2B (3.10 Å). Thr52/D side-chain OG1 engaged O1A (3.32 Å).

Multiple electrostatic interactions and salt bridges further anchored the ligand. The side chain of Arg6/D donated to O3′ (NE…O3′, 3.38 Å; NH2…O3′, 3.64 Å) and to O2A (NH2…O2A, 3.81 Å). Arg203/D side-chain NH2 formed a salt bridge to O2A (3.11 Å), and NH1 and NH2 to O3B (3.23 Å and 3.85 Å, respectively). The side-chain NH1 and NH2 of Arg156/D also contacted O3G (3.67 Å and 3.39 Å, respectively). No negatively charged protein atom was found within salt-bridge distance of AGS in this monomer.

Hydrophobic and van der Waals contacts surrounded the ligand binding site. Pro202/D made four contacts with a closest approach of 3.66 Å, and Pro7/D made three contacts at 3.55 Å. Ile174/D contributed two hydrophobic interactions at 4.20 Å. Gly49/D and Leu48/D were positioned at 4.06 Å and 4.36 Å, respectively, from nonpolar regions of AGS. No aromatic π–π stacking or water-mediated hydrogen bonds were observed.
